Who I Am

I'm a Data Science and Actuaría student at ITAM (Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México), where I hold an Academic Excellence Scholarship — awarded to the top-performing students.

My academic journey took me to Barcelona, Spain, where I completed an exchange semester at the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC), one of Europe's leading technical universities. That experience sharpened both my technical skills and my global perspective.
